=== PIECE_PROPERTIES (d20) ===
1. Requires a rare or exotic material from a distant location.
2. Involves knowledge lost to time; must be researched or recovered.
3. Needs the aid of a skilled specialist or craftsman.
4. Tied to a ritual or ceremony that must be performed correctly.
5. Involves a significant sacrifice—time, gold, or something personal.
6. Connected to a specific place of power or historical significance.
7. Requires the blessing or permission of an authority figure.
8. Involves a creature, ally, or contact who must be convinced or recruited.
9. Tied to a seasonal event, celestial alignment, or auspicious date.
10. Requires overcoming a trial, challenge, or test of worthiness.
11. Involves recovering something stolen, lost, or hidden long ago.
12. Connected to a bloodline, oath, or inherited obligation.
13. Requires collaboration with an unusual or unexpected ally.
14. Tied to a secret that must be discovered or revealed.
15. Involves a dangerous journey or expedition to obtain it.
16. Requires appeasing or bargaining with a powerful entity.
17. Connected to a past failure that must be redeemed or corrected.
18. Involves a creative or artistic component—song, craft, or ritual art.
19. Tied to the death or passing of something significant.
20. Requires uniting disparate parties or reconciling old conflicts.

=== COMPLICATIONS (d12) ===
1. A rival or enemy seeks the same piece or aims to obstruct you.
2. Time pressure: a deadline or window of opportunity is closing.
3. The cost or requirement has increased unexpectedly.
4. A key contact, ally, or resource has become unavailable.
5. New information reveals the piece is harder to obtain than expected.
6. Your actions have drawn unwanted attention from powerful forces.
7. A previous choice or commitment now conflicts with this goal.
8. The piece is guarded, hidden, or claimed by another party.
9. A moral or ethical dilemma complicates the path forward.
10. Physical or logistical barriers block access to what you need.
11. Trust has been broken; an ally or source has betrayed or deceived you.
12. The piece itself carries a curse, cost, or unintended consequence.
